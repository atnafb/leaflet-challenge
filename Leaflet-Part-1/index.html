FAQ

  • What is Tectonic?

    Tectonic refers to the large-scale processes that shape the Earth's surface, including the movement of tectonic plates that cause earthquakes, volcanic activity, and mountain formation.

  • What is Depth?

    Depth refers to how far below the Earth's surface the earthquake occurs, measured in kilometers (km).

  • What is Magnitude?

    Magnitude is a measure of the energy released during an earthquake, often quantified using the Richter scale or Moment Magnitude scale.
